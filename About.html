Our Story

Our journey began in early 2023 with a simple idea and a passion for great coffee. We started by crafting "Kaffin in a Bottle" in our home in Mamatid, Cabuyao, Laguna. This unique concept allowed us to share our love for coffee with friends and family, bottling up joy in each batch. Our home-based venture quickly grew, and we found ourselves pouring our hearts into each bottle, carefully blending flavors that resonated with the warmth and comfort we wanted to bring to our community.

To share our passion beyond bottles, we began the Coffee Cart Event services. Our first customers were our supportive family members, who believed in our dream from the very beginning. Their unending encouragement fueled our ambition and gratitude, motivating us to perfect our craft. These events were more than just coffee services; they became opportunities to connect with people, share stories, and build relationships, all centered around our shared love for coffee.

By early 2024, our dream took a significant leap forward with the opening of our first physical shop in Banadero, Calamba, Laguna. Old Coast Cafe was born out of a desire to create a welcoming space where people could enjoy quality coffee and meaningful conversations. As we look to the future, we are excited to expand Old Coast Cafe to new locations in Cabuyao and across Laguna and Manila. Our goal is to create a place where everyone feels at home, whether they're grabbing a quick coffee or spending a leisurely afternoon. Our commitment to our mission—to bring happiness through a cup of coffee and to let coffee connect us—remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our Values

At Old Coast Cafe, our values are the foundation of everything we do. They guide our actions, shape our culture, and define our commitment to our customers and community.

Quality

We believe that great coffee starts with great ingredients. That's why we meticulously select the finest coffee beans and ingredients to ensure every cup meets our high standards. From sourcing to brewing, we are dedicated to providing exceptional quality in every product we offer.

Community

Old Coast Cafe is more than just a coffee shop; it's a place where people come together. We cherish the connections we build with our customers, neighbors, and local businesses.

Sustainability

We recognize our responsibility to the environment and are committed to sustainable practices. From using eco-friendly packaging to supporting fair trade coffee, we make conscious choices to reduce our environmental footprint.

Customer Service

At Old Coast Cafe, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We believe in creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels valued and appreciated. Our team is passionate about coffee and dedicated to providing personalized service.
